FAQ

Questions, answered

Do I need any coding or AI experience?

None at all. The course assumes you've never written a line of code and don't know what an LLM is. Everything is taught from scratch, in plain English.

How much time does it take?

It's designed for a few hours a week over six months. You can go faster or slower — your progress is saved, so you can pick up anytime.

Do I need to install anything?

No. The course runs in any web browser, and the coding lessons use free, browser-based tools (Google Colab). Nothing to download.

Is it really beginner-friendly?

Yes. Each lesson uses everyday analogies, one clear example, and a small hands-on task. If a word is new, it's explained right there.

Do I get a certificate?

Yes — when you complete all lessons, you can generate and print a personalized certificate of completion.

Will this get me a job immediately?

It builds the foundation, honestly. Six months gets you understanding AI, writing code, and building small projects. The final lesson maps the exact next steps toward landing a Forward Deployed Engineer role.
